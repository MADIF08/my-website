Welcome to My Website

Philippine Indigenous Communities are the original inhabitants of the archipelago, known for their rich traditions, unique cultures, and deep connection to nature. Spread across mountains, forests, and islands, they preserve ancient practices and ancestral knowledge passed down through generations. Despite modern challenges, they continue to protect their heritage, identity, and ancestral lands.
